ZAiV-MHB

ZAiV-MHB

Overview

This is the documentation for ZAiV-MHB.

ZAiV-MHB is high performace Edge AI Board that can be applied to various fields through its outstanding AI system.

In particular, the size of Edge AI is very small while maintaining high performance targeting edge board.

Specifications

  • System Module
    • NXP i.MX8M Application Processor
      • 4x Cortex-A53 up to 1.8 GHz
      • Cortex-M7 up to 800 MHz
      • 32-bit DDR4 and LPDDR4 up to 4.0GT/s
      • 1 x MIPI CSI
      • Video Decode: 1080p60, h.265/4, VP9, VP8
      • Video Encode: 1080p60, h.265/4
      • GPU: 16 GFLOPS (high-precision) OpenGL® ES 3.1/3.0, Vulkan®, Open CL™ 1.2 FP, OpenVG™ 1.1
    • Hailo 8 AI Processor
      • Unprecedented AI compute power of 26 Tera-Operations Per Second (TOPS)
      • Best-in-class power efficiency with typical power consumption of 2.5W
      • Enabling real-time, low latency & high-efficiency AI inferencing on edge devices
    • Interfaces
      • 1x Gigabit Ethernet (Supported POE)
      • 1x USB Type-C (USB 2.0)
      • Extention IO
        • Digital Input: 4ea (24V NPN Isolated)
        • Digital Output: 4ea (24V NPN Isolated)
        • UART Console: 1ea
        • PWM (For Light Brightness Control)
